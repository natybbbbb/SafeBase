# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Use welcoming and inclusive language, respect differing viewpoints, and accept constructive criticism.

## Enforcement
Report unacceptable behavior to maintainers at conduct@safebase.example. Actions may include warnings or removal from the community.

## Attribution
Based on widely adopted community standards and adapted for SafeBase.
